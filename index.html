Geography Awareness Week with OpenStreetMap!

Teachers, students, community groups, map lovers, in the US and around the world, join together to celebrate geography and make maps with OpenStreetMap, the free and openly editable map of the world. Sign up for an event near you or plan your own mapathon. You can contribute to food mapping projects around the world. Get in touch, we're happy to help!
